Monterey
Monterey is a city located in Monterey County on the southern edge of Monterey Bay on the U.S. state of California’s Central Coast. It was founded on June 3, 1770, and functioned as the capital of Alta California under both Spain (1804–1821) and Mexico (1822–1846). Monterey was also California’s first theater host during this period. Monterey Bay is known for its scenic beauty, history and wildlife.
